Embark on an Adventure...

    You stumble across a strange and mysterious website. Your curiousity is piqued by the shades of teal splashing onto your screen, an air of adventure seems to flow about this place. As you read the paragraph before you, a thought enters your mind, "Where is this going?"

    Before you can ponder the subject any longer, you are given an answer in the form of another question: Where do you want this to go? This is the question that all travellers who are fortunate enough to venture into this uncharted territory must answer.
